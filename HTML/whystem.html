Why Stem?

STEM is vital to our world. It gives us in-depth learning about our surroundings. Without STEM, we would have less doctors, higher death rates, and less detection of certain diseases. As technology continues to expand, we need more intuitive minds to assist with discovering more technologies which will benefit our world!
